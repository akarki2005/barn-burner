Barn Burner

Barn Burner (noun - informal, Canadian): An event, typically a hockey game, that is very exciting or intense.

About

Barn Burner is an air hockey game created in the Godot game engine. Currently in the alpha stage of development, the game supports local multiplayer between two players, with 32 unique playable teams in the game, inspired by the 32 teams in the NHL. Check out the "builds" section of this webpage to give it a try!

Roadmap

I have lots planned over the next few months to improve upon the game, to hopefully (maybe?) reach a point where I can consider the game complete. Below is a non-exhaustive snapshot of the features and improvements that I plan on implementing to the game in the future before a full release.

  • Boost mechanic (either auto-regenerating or boost pads, haven't decided yet)
    • Powershot when puck is hit while boosted
    • Bodycheck when player is hit while boosted
  • Power-ups
    • Temporarily slow opponent
    • Temporarily shrink opponent
    • Temporarily increase own speed
    • Temporarily increase own size
  • Sound effects
    • Ingame sounds (for shots, bodychecks, goals, etc.)
    • UI sounds
    • Title screen soundtrack?
  • Art improvements
    • Revamp title screen
    • Convert pause/options menu to pixel art
    • Standardize pixel size across game sprites
  • Modes
    • Singleplayer (season mode, playoffs mode etc.)
    • Online Multiplayer (maybe)
